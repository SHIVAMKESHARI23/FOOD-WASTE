Food is a necessary substance in human life and provides essential nutrients, protein, vitamins, carbs, fat, and minerals, which are necessary for life. We all know the importance of food, but in recent times global food waste of billion tonnes become an issue worldwide especially in developed, developing nations, and metropolitan cities. The more humans change their way of living, the more they lose and waste food. According to the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) Global Food Waste Report 2021, globally over 1 billion tonnes of food is wasted per year, 13% comes from retail, 26% from food service, and 61% from households. China ranked top in food waste with 91.6 million tonnes followed by India with 68.8 million tonnes, the USA with 19.4 million tonnes, and many other countries.
